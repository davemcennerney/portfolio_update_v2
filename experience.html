Experience

So, in this section you can find out more about me; what my past experiences are, and my hopes and dreams for the future. As mentioned on my Home page I have been in the military for the past 12 years. I am currently in the Royal Marines Band Service as a Trumpet and Violin player. I have had a wonderful career so far in a great organistaion that has taught me a lot and given me many valuable experiences, as well as meeting a lot of great people. I have travelled to many parts of the world which I would otherwise not have visited and played music in many of the great concert halls across the globe. I have gained leadership experience and it has made me understand the importance of working as a team. I have very fond memories of my time in the military and will be sad to leave, but for me it is time to follow a new path.
